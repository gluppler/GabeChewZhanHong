About Me

My passion for security didn't start in a classroom; it began 20 years ago with a relentless curiosity to understand how physical systems work by taking them apart. This deep, hands-on journey into hardware, firmware, and signal manipulation has given me a unique perspective on real-world attack surfaces.

Now, as a final-year Computer Science student at Sunway University, I am formalizing this deep, practical knowledge and applying it to the next generation of security challenges. I am driven by the challenge of understanding how systems break and using that knowledge to build more resilient defenses.
