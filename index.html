About Me

20 Years of Hands-On Security

From taking apart physical systems to securing the next generation of digital infrastructure

My passion for security began 20 years ago, not in a classroom, but with a relentless curiosity to understand how physical systems work by taking them apart. Now a final-year Computer Science student at Sunway University, I am formalizing this deep, practical knowledge to tackle the next generation of security challenges.

I am the founder of Behelit Systems, a security firm dedicated to securing the physical assets that traditional cybersecurity overlooks, and Cult of the LOLCOW (cLc), Malaysia's first and only open-source hardware hacking community. These platforms work in synergy: cLc drives open research and education, while Behelit Systems applies that knowledge to solve complex commercial security problems.
